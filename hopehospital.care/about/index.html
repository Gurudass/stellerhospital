Overview

Welcome to Hope Hospital – A Unit of Tulasi Healthcare, where a unique reality was born from the vision of four extraordinary doctors. With over a decade of dedicated service, they aimed to create a healthcare system that goes beyond merely treating physical needs. Our hospital is a haven of timely, efficient, and top-quality care. Equipped with advanced medical technology, diagnostic instruments, and state-of-the-art facilities, we ensure accurate diagnoses and effective treatments. From outpatient to inpatient care, we constantly embrace cutting-edge medical advancements, making us your trusted destination for comprehensive healthcare. Experience the difference at Hope Hospital, where compassion meets innovation.

Core Values

At Hope Hospital – A Unit of Tulasi Healthcare in KR Puram, Bangalore, our core values guide our exceptional care and transformative healthcare experience.

Every patient is approached with empathy and comprehension, and treated with the uttermost care, respect, and compassion.

Excellence: We endeavor for excellence in all aspects of healthcare, from our highly trained physicians to our cutting-edge facilities, to ensure the utmost quality of care.

Innovation: We embrace cutting-edge medical advancements and technologies and are continually on the lookout for innovative ways to provide the best possible outcomes for our patients.

Integrity: We adhere to the highest ethical standards, nurturing trust and openness in our relationships with patients, employees, and members of the community.

We believe in the power of collaboration and cooperation, collaborating closely with patients, families, and healthcare professionals to achieve the best possible outcomes.

Patient-Centered Approach: Our patients are the focal point of all that we do. Individualized care is provided with a focus on the patient’s requirements, preferences, and general well-being.

Community Engagement: We actively engage with the community to promote health education, awareness, and preventive care, thereby extending our hospital’s positive influence beyond its walls.

The pursuance of holistic, patient-centered, and transformative healthcare experiences for all those who entrust us with their health is guided by these core values.

Frequently Asked Questions

Have a question Scroll through the list of frequently asked questions but if you dont find your answer, feel free to give us a call or drop us a note. Find out More

What are the services offered by the hospital?

Ans: Our hospital offers a wide range of medical services including diagnosis, treatment,
and rehabilitation for various illnesses and injuries. We have specialized departments for
Emergency, Critical Care, cardiology, neurology, orthopedics, gynecology, pediatrics,
oncology, and more

What are the timings of the hospital?

Ans: Our hospital operates 24/7, and all departments are open round the clock.

Is there an emergency department in the hospital?

Ans: Yes, we have a well-equipped emergency department that operates 24/7 to provide
medical assistance to patients in need.

Is there an ICU in the hospital?

Ans: Yes, we have a well-equipped Intensive Care Unit (ICU) to provide critical care to
patients.

What are the visiting hours for patients?

Ans: Our hospital allows visitors from 10 am to 8 pm daily. However, visiting hours for ICU patients may be restricted for the patient’s well-being.

How many beds are available in the hospital?

Ans: Our hospital has 75 beds, including general, private, and critical care beds.

Can I book an appointment online?

Ans: Yes, you can book an appointment online through our website or by calling our
appointment desk.
